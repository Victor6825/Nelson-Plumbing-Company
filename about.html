OUR STORY

Founded in 2077 during the Great Water Crisis, Nelson Plumbing emerged from the ashes of outdated infrastructure and failing legacy systems. Our founder, A. N. Droid, envisioned a future where water management was seamless, efficient, and intelligent.

What began as a small team of visionary engineers has grown into the industry leader in advanced fluid dynamics. Our proprietary Quantum Flow™ technology has revolutionized how water systems are designed, monitored, and maintained.
